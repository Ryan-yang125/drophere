# Community code of conduct

DropHere welcomes people who contribute code, documentation, tests, bug
reports, and deployment knowledge.

Participate with respect. Give feedback about observable work, share enough
context for others to reproduce a problem, and leave room for different levels
of experience. Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, and the
publication of another person's credentials or private data are unacceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove content and restrict participation when needed
to protect the community. Report sensitive conduct concerns through the
repository's private security-reporting channel.
